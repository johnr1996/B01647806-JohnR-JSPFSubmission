PORTFOLIO

Car-Toons - Work In Progress

This is an ongoing 2-person group project that I am part of.
Car-Toons is an arcade kart racing game, with a focus on car customisation
and tuning, heavily inspired by 60s cartoons like Looney Tunes and Wacky Races.




Beached

This is my latest project, Beached, a survival-simulation game inspired by Castaway.
The player must survive as long as possible having washed ashore after a shipwreck,
and must explore the desert island to find food and resources that will allow them to
craft tools, shelters, and eventually a raft to escape.
